 \textbf{Question}:

 
\noindent If in $\triangle ABC$ , $\vec{BA}=2a$ and $\vec{BC}=3b$ ,then $\vec{AC}$ is 
\underline{\hspace{3cm}}
\textbf{Solution}:
 
$\vec{CB}=-3b$

 $\vec{BA} +\vec{CB}+\vec{AC} =0$
 
 $ 2a +\brak{-3b}+\vec{AC} =0$
 
 $\implies \vec{AC}=3b-2a$
